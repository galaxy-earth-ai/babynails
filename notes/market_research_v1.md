## 2. Service Menu: High-End Vancouver Studio

*Targeting: Downtown/Yaletown/Kitsilano demographics. All prices in CAD.*

### **Nail Services**
*   **Signature Gel Manicure ($75+)**: Detailed cuticle care, shaping, and high-quality gel polish application. Includes a hand massage with organic oils.
*   **Luxury Spa Pedicure ($95+)**: Relaxing soak, exfoliation, callous treatment, and hydrating mask followed by gel polish.
*   **Gel-X Extensions ($120+)**: The "gold standard" of extensions. Lightweight, durable, and natural-looking full-cover extensions.
*   **Apres Gel Sculpting ($140+)**: Custom sculpted hard gel for maximum length and strength.
*   **Artist Tier Nail Art ($25 - $60)**: Custom hand-painted designs, 3D elements, or complex chrome/gradient work.

### **Eyelash Services**
*   **Natural Classic Set ($150)**: 1:1 application for a refined, "mascara-only" look.
*   **Signature Hybrid Set ($185)**: A blend of classic and volume for added texture and fluffiness.
*   **Grand Volume Set ($220)**: Multi-lash fans for a dramatic, dense, and glamorous look.
*   **Lash Lift & Tint ($110)**: For those preferring their natural lashes; lifts and darkens for 6-8 weeks.
*   **Keratin Lash Infusion ($130)**: Premium lift treatment that strengthens and repairs natural lashes.

## 3. Retail Pricing Strategy

To maintain a "High-End" brand image while leveraging low dropshipping costs, a **4x to 6x markup** is recommended for retail.

| Tier | Category | Wholesale Cost (CAD) | Recommended Retail (CAD) | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Essential** | Minimalist/Solid Colors | $2.50 - $4.00 | **$20.00 - $25.00** | Competitive with drugstores but "curated" quality. |
| **Boutique** | Art/Chrome/Cat-Eye | $4.00 - $7.00 | **$35.00 - $45.00** | Professional look for "in-between" salon visits. |
| **Couture** | 3D/Rhinestones/Luxury | $7.00 - $12.00 | **$65.00 - $85.00** | Positioned as a luxury "event" nail (e.g., weddings). |

**Bundling Strategy:**
*   "The Jetsetter Kit": 1 set of nails + application kit (glue, file, buffer) for a flat $5.00 add-on.
*   "In-Salon Upsell": Offer a 20% discount on a retail set if purchased during a service.
